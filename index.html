Process

How Lab Digitiser Works

From field sample to digital record in four simple steps.

1

Admin Setup

Admin configures companies, plants, sample locations, parameters, and user accounts on the web platform.

2

Field Sampling

Operators collect physical samples from assigned plant zones: inlet, outlet, aeration tank, sump, and more.

3

App Submission

Using the mobile app, operators enter PH, TDS, TSS, COD, BOD, Hardness readings in the 4-step wizard.

4

Analyse and Export

Admins review the dashboard, run reports, and export consolidated CSV data for compliance and review.
